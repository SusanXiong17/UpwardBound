About Me

Hello, my name is Susan Xiong. I am an incoming senior at Edison High School. I have lots of sisters with no brothers and I am the third youngest of my family. I enjoy eating good food and spending long, stressfree days relaxing and doing the thing I love.

One of my hobbies is to draw which ranges from anything cartoony to still life and realistic drawings. I am working more towards realistic drawings and hope to start getting into other types of art like watercolor and painting.
